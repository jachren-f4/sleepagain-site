What the app does

Seven nights of data is enough to see your pattern. Thirty nights is enough to understand it.

01 — Reads your data

From Apple Health, on device

Connects to Apple Health. Reads HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep stage history. All processing stays on your device — nothing is sent to external servers.

02 — Finds the pattern

What precedes your bad nights

After 7 nights, the app maps what leads to early waking — elevated evening HR, low HRV the day before, alcohol, stress markers. Your pattern, not a generic one.

03 — Predicts tonight

Before you go to sleep

Each evening you get a risk score. On a high-risk night, you know in advance — and you have time to act on it before it becomes a 3am problem.
