01 Why I wrote this

Twenty years of building. Two years of putting my sleep back together.

By
Joakim Achrén
Co-founded Next Games, acquired by Netflix. Author of Long Term Game. Helsinki.

I built companies for twenty years. Then I invested in them for six. The 2019 burnout was the obvious break.

The 2023 collapse was different. I was not running a company at the time. I was rested on paper. But I would wake at 3am with my chest tight and my mind running pricing scenarios for businesses that did not exist yet.

I read every sleep book I could find. I tried the trackers, the apps, the supplements, the routines. Most of them are written by sleep researchers for sleep clinic patients. They are not wrong. They just do not know what it is like to be the one with the company on your back.

So I wrote the book I needed. Two years of reading and testing. It is the manual for founders who have sacrificed sleep for the company and want it back without giving up the company.

03 Inside the book

A field manual, not a textbook.

  • I.

    The mind that won't switch off
