Sleep Again

Finding Rest in a World Built on Urgency

by Joakim Achrén

A practical guide for entrepreneurs who've sacrificed sleep for success, and are ready to reclaim it without sacrificing their ambitions.

Why This Book?

You've read the sleep tips. You know about blue light and caffeine. But as an entrepreneur, your sleep problems run deeper: racing minds at 3 AM, the weight of decisions, the fear of missing out on opportunities.

Science-Based

Understand the hormonal symphony that governs your sleep and why entrepreneurial stress disrupts it differently.

Entrepreneur-Focused

Strategies designed for the unique challenges of building something from nothing, while still getting rest.

Practical Tools

From sleep trackers to meditation techniques, build a personalized toolkit that fits your lifestyle.
