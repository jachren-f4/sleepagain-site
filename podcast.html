About Me

I'm Joakim Achrén. I've been building companies for over 20 years, including a game studio that was acquired by Netflix. I spent 6 years investing in startups. Now I build AI products.

After a burnout in 2019, my sleep completely broke down. I spent two years researching why entrepreneurs specifically sleep worse than everyone else. That research became Sleep Again: Finding Rest in a World Built on Urgency, published April 9, 2026.

About the Book

Sleep Again is the first sleep book written specifically for entrepreneurs and founders. It combines sleep science with first-person experience from 20 years of building companies. 27 chapters, 54,000 words, 87 endnotes from peer-reviewed research.

The book covers hormones and circadian rhythms, caffeine and alcohol timing, overtraining, chronotypes, mental strategies for 3am wake-ups, and a practical sleep toolkit that costs less than $50.

Topics I Can Discuss

  • Why founders are uniquely bad at sleep (and why generic advice fails them)
  • The founder insomnia cycle: stress, poor sleep, worse decisions, more stress
  • Positive stress and sleep: why excitement wrecks rest as much as anxiety
  • Chronotypes for entrepreneurs: Lion, Bear, Wolf, Dolphin scheduling
  • Overtraining and 4am wake-ups: the hidden link between the gym and insomnia
  • The $50 sleep setup that outperformed a $3,000 smart mattress
  • Caffeine timing, alcohol protocols, and meal strategies
  • The Mental Bouncer Technique for falling back asleep
  • Building an evening wind-down routine for unpredictable schedules
  • Sleep and decision-making: the cognitive cost of running a company on 5 hours
